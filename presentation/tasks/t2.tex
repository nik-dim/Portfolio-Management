    \framesubtitle{\texttt{palladium} }
    \begin{itemize}

    	\item Secondary product from mining operations of other metals
    	\item As miners have less control over the extracted quantities, demand outstrips supply
    	\item Price has doubled over the last year
    \end{itemize}
